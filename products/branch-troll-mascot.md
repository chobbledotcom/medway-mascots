## Branch Troll Mascot Hire

The Branch Troll mascot costume is available for hire in Gravesend, Chatham, Istead Rise, Meopham, and surrounding areas. This character costume is based on the Branch character from the Troll movie franchise.

The costume fits adults up to 5.9ft tall. It is suitable for children's birthday parties, community events, and school functions.
