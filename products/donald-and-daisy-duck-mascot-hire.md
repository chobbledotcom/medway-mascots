## Donald and Daisy Duck Mascot Hire

Our Donald and Daisy Duck mascot costumes are available for hire at children's birthday parties, school events, and family celebrations. These popular Disney characters provide entertainment and photo opportunities for guests of all ages.

## Costume Specifications

Both Donald and Daisy Duck costumes accommodate adults up to 5ft 9in in height. The costumes are designed for comfortable wear throughout your event and feature authentic Disney character styling.

## Hire Options

These costumes are available for self-hire only. You can collect the costumes from our base location or arrange delivery alongside other party equipment such as bouncy castles. Delivery is available across Kent and South East London.
