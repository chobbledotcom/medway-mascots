The Hello Kitty costume accommodates adults between slim and large build. The outfit includes the full character head and body suit, providing the recognisable appearance of the Sanrio character.

Hello Kitty works well for children's birthday parties, particularly for younger children who enjoy the gentle, friendly character. The costume is also suitable for themed events, nursery celebrations, and community gatherings.
