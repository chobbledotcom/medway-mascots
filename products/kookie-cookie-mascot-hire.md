## Kookie Cookie Mascot Costume

The Kookie Cookie mascot costume features the beloved Shopkins character at birthday parties and events throughout Kent. This costume can interact with party guests, join in activities, and create memorable moments during celebrations.

## Hire Details

The costume costs £35 to hire. A £35 refundable deposit is required when collecting the costume to cover any potential damage. The deposit is returned when the costume comes back in its original condition.

Delivery is available alongside bouncy castle bookings or can be arranged separately for an additional fee. Alternatively, you can collect and return the costume directly from our address.

## What's Included

The hire includes the complete Kookie Cookie costume suitable for adult wearers. The costume covers greeting activities, party game participation, and special appearances during celebrations. The mascot can be hired independently or combined with other party equipment.

## Service Areas

We serve Gravesend, Chatham, Rochester, Gillingham, Longfield, Maidstone, Medway, Tonbridge, and surrounding Kent areas.
