## Mickey & Minnie Mascot Costumes for Hire

Our Mickey and Minnie mascot costumes are available for hire at children's parties, corporate events, school fairs, and community celebrations. These classic Disney characters provide entertainment and photo opportunities at events across Kent.

## Costume Details

The Mickey and Minnie costumes are suitable for adults up to 5ft 9in in height. Both costumes are available for self-hire or can be provided with a professional performer to entertain guests. The high-quality materials ensure durability throughout your event.

## Service Areas

We deliver Mickey and Minnie mascot costumes to events across Dartford, Bexleyheath, Welling, Sidcup, Medway, Maidstone, Sevenoaks, Tonbridge, Gravesend, and surrounding areas in Kent and South East London.
